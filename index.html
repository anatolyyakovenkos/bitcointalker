What is Binance Coin (BNB)?
 BNB is the native utility token of the Binance exchange. It can be used to pay trading fees at a discount, participate in token sales on Binance Launchpad, and much more. Every quarter, Binance uses 20% of profits to buy back and burn BNB tokens, reducing supply permanently.

Token Supply:
Total Supply: 200,000,000 BNB
Circulating: ~114,000,000 BNB
Quarterly burns reduce supply over time
Target: reduce to 100,000,000 BNB eventually
